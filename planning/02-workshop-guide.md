## Opening (5 minutes)

Say this to frame the session:

> "The goal today is not to plan the website. The goal is to understand the business deeply enough that every decision we make about the website is grounded in what customers actually need and what Vitorra is actually selling. Everything I hear today will be converted into a requirements document that drives the entire build. Nothing gets designed until this is done."

## Closing (10 minutes)

Ask this at the end:

> "Before we close — is there anything about how Vitorra operates, what customers say, or what the business is trying to achieve that I haven't asked about? Anything that feels important and wasn't covered?"

Leave space. This is often where the most important information comes out.
